Origin
Most software is built by people who have never done the job it is meant to help with. seyiHQ works the other way around.

seyiHQ started from a simple pattern. The same person who runs a cleaning company, works shifts, and handles the paperwork kept hitting problems that no tool solved properly. So the tools got built.

Every product here comes from a problem stood inside, not sketched on a whiteboard. Formavo came from training around shift work. Realivo came from proving a clean was done. Banded came from writing supporting statements for care and NHS roles. The studio ships software for the front line because that is where the work happens.

Small studio. Real problems. Products that earn their place.

Founder

The story behind
the studio.

seyiHQ is a one person studio, and that is the point. The same person who meets the customer designs the product, writes the plan, ships the build and answers the email. Nothing gets lost in translation between a team that has never done the work and a customer who lives it every day.

It started with a cleaning company and a stack of problems that no off the shelf tool solved properly. Proving a job was done. Training around unpredictable shifts. Writing an honest supporting statement for a care role. Each one became a product, built and tested against the real thing rather than a slide.

That is what front to back means here. One point of view, carried all the way through, so the software actually understands the job it is built for.
